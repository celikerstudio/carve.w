## Artikel Generatie Prompt

### Batch Prompt (10 articles at a time)

```
You are a fitness wiki writer for Carve, a fitness app. Write wiki articles in English.

**Style:**
- Short and concise (300-500 words per article)
- Evidence-based where possible
- Practical and actionable
- Understandable for beginners, useful for advanced users
- No clickbait - just the term as title

**Structure per article:**

## What is [term]?
[Short definition, 2-3 sentences]

:::takeaway
The most important insight about this topic with **key numbers** highlighted.
:::

## How does it work?
[Explanation of the mechanism/principle]

## Practical application
[Concrete tips, numbers, guidelines]

:::checklist
- First tip title | Detailed explanation of the first tip.
- Second tip title | Detailed explanation of the second tip.
- Third tip title | Detailed explanation of the third tip.
:::

## Common mistakes
[2-3 mistakes to avoid as bullet points]

**Special syntax:**
- :::takeaway ... ::: = Highlighted key insight card (use once per article)
- :::checklist ... ::: = Checklist with checkmarks (format: "- Title | Description")
- [[slug]] = Wiki link to another article
- **bold** = Bold text for emphasis

**Wiki links - use [[slug]] syntax to link to other articles:**
Available: [[creatine]], [[protein]], [[calories]], [[hypertrophy]], [[progressive-overload]], [[volume]], [[intensity]], [[sleep]], [[overtraining]], etc.

**Output as SQL INSERT statement:**

INSERT INTO wiki_articles (slug, title, category, tags, language, content_markdown, summary, evidence_rating, author, is_published, view_count)
VALUES
('slug', 'Title', 'Category',
 ARRAY['tag1', 'tag2', 'tag3'], 'en',
'## What is [term]?

Definition here...

Key insight with **important numbers** here.
:::

Explanation here...

Tips here...

## Common mistakes

- Mistake one

',
'One sentence summary.',
'well-established', 'Carve Team', true, 0);

- 'well-established' = lots of research, widely accepted
```

## Taal Strategie

### Besluit: Engels Eerst

| Aspect | Engels eerst | Nederlands eerst |
|--------|--------------|------------------|
| AI kwaliteit | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ Beter | ⭐⭐⭐ Okee |
| Vertaling | EN→NL is makkelijk | NL→EN lastiger |
| Markt | Internationaal | Alleen NL/BE |
| Wiki links | Werkt in beide | Werkt in beide |

### Praktische Aanpak

1. **Fase 1:** Schrijf alle 100 artikelen in **Engels** (`language: 'en'`)
2. **Fase 2:** Vertaal naar Nederlands met AI (`language: 'nl'`)
3. **Fase 3:** App toont juiste taal op basis van device/user setting

### Slug Strategie

- Slugs blijven **Engels** voor beide talen
- Voorbeelden: `creatine`, `sleep`, `volume`, `protein`
- Alleen de content en titel verschillen per taal

### Database Support

Het `language` veld in `wiki_articles` ondersteunt 'nl' en 'en'.

```sql
-- Engels artikel
INSERT INTO wiki_articles (slug, title, language, ...)
VALUES ('sleep', 'Sleep', 'en', ...);

-- Nederlands artikel (zelfde slug)
INSERT INTO wiki_articles (slug, title, language, ...)
VALUES ('sleep', 'Slaap', 'nl', ...);
```

### App Implementatie

```swift
// WikiService.fetchArticles filter op language
let deviceLanguage = Locale.current.language.languageCode?.identifier ?? "en"
let articles = try await WikiService.shared.fetchArticles(language: deviceLanguage)
```
